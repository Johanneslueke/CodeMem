Welcome to CodeMem

CodeMem's purpose is to implement a memory management system in a multi-threaded enviroment via c++. The reason for this is to learn/improve necessary coding-skills. The whole thing right now is a one man show but if you feel like you want to contribute to it or just want to do your own thing feel free to fork the project or to push updates to CodeMem.

It is up to you!
